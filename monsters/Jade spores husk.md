### **Jade-Husk (Lesser)**   

A Lesser Jade-Husk represents a miner who has only recently succumbed to the "Glint-Sickness." The obsidian hasn't fully replaced their skeleton yet, making them faster but more fragile than their elder counterparts. They are often found in the shallower veins of the Emerald Drift, still clutching the rotted remains of their original gear.
