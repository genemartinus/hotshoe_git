Everyone has a reason to visit the public stone lined well at the center of the hamlet.  Ash falls make the surface water unpredictable for drinking so the public well is the only choice. 

## Hooks and opportunities

- [[Orion]], [[Kip]] and [[Durn]] are not very social and prefer to get water when others are not around.  They prefer to stay out of sight and that's tolerated because they have not presented a danger or reason to be feared.  [[Glour]], [[Obur]] and [[Stefania]] find them useful informants because they travel the volcanic wastes.
- [[Millicent]] likes to take samples of the well water for her interests in clean water and apothecary needs. 
- [[Dimble]] needs to draw a lot of water to provide food and drink at the [[Thirsty Pebble]].
- [[Kaelen]] needs water to prepare [[Cinder-berry bush]]s to make [[Cinder berry jam]]. 
- [[Phanzis]] needs water for his gardening project.
- [[Milton]] needs water for his apple trees
