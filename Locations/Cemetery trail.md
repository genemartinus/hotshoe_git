Cemetery trail is named because it crests the basin ridge at the dwarven cemetery.  Aside from access to the cemetery, the trail connects to the South Conniption road and is passable by foot given the steep descent.  

Cemetery trail also features access to the glaciers on the sides of the volcano, volcanic wasteland where occasional obsidian shards can be found, [[Blue fang salt lake]] and the entrance to Hotshoe where the town's cemetery is located.

This is where the [[Soil type vegetation relationships]] become visible and maybe troubling.
