[[Milton]] has a pig pen behind his [[Kick ash mercantile]] market.  

 It is unusual because he's trying to raise the pigs using local sources of fresh feed: [[Ash yam]], [[Botanical guide#^da780d|needle grass]], scraps from the [[Thirsty Pebble]] and whatever else the neighbors want to contribute.  Hotshoe residents know that having local livestock is important and willingly cooperate even though Milton still charges full price no matter who buys his pork products.  Some think this isn't fair if they provide more for the pigs while Milton sees it as an egalitarian stance so everyone can afford it, not just those who have more to give.  The real money potential comes from the experimental leather made with alternative curing and tanning techniques.  The hides seem to be more durable and resist wear better than pigs raised in other locations without a diet of [[Ash yam]].  [[Milton]] imagines leather cured with a tanning solution infused with the ingredients from the local flora and fauna might perform beyond expectations of typical leathers.  If the players can convince [[Milton]] to trust them with his secret project they could offer to help Milton collect new materials for his investigation.  This would be a quest line to come back with hard to find ingredients that require a scavenger hunt out into the volcanic wasteland, maybe with the help of Orion's team.  In exchange he may give them some sample leather to test in the field.
