Grey wash creek is aptly named because it carries the grey ash laden water off Mt Conniption's flanks and down valley.  On sunny days the glacier melt may prevail an the water runs clear(er) and gives Hotshoe some reprieve from the turbulent past.

The creek is part of the mystery of the water in Hotshoe.  The [[Hotshoe public well]] is the obvious natural source since the creek isn't entirely reliable source for potable water.
