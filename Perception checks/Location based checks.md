#### Cemetery
- Road/path to the cemetery has to permit Jube to do her work hauling bodies and she might need some help through the ash.  
- What is the quality of the landscaping?
- Who helps Jube with the cemetery?
- Any evidence of Kaelen's actions or ambitions
- What is the condition of the ash here?
- Any talk or evidence of holy water
- Where are Jube's tools?  There's a bedroll in the toolshed
- Does Kaelen sleep/live/worship in the tool shed; where is his altar and harvest products.
- Is Kaelen present?
- NPC encounters
	- Dimble
	- Jube - Jube is doing her cemetery thing depending on time of day.
	- Mellicent - Might come to the cemetery if asked to investigate the fungus.
	- Phanzis - Might be here to pay his respects to someone interred there.
	- Yingman - Might come to admire the gravestones because that's part of his history. Same with the other dwarves.  They may be perturbed with Kaelen's scraping.
	- Stefania - Doesn't come here except to put down the zombie uprising.
	- Glour - The dark night watchman keeps an eye on everything including the cemetery and maybe Kaelen's business.
	- Obur - Wouldn't be here unless needed/called for.
	- Kaelen - Landscaping when he is here.  He keeps trimming the same grass/bushes over and over.
#### After curfew with Glour
- This can be any time when it is dark and being outside after curfew is unadvisable, especially if you are trying to learn secrets or spy on someone/thing.  Perception checks are to evaluate who sees who.
- Patrons relations
	- Dimble - Safe at home except for stories and gossip that happen when he's not around.
	- Jube - Might be out at night with a good reason if there is one.
	- Mellicent - Home safe
	- Phanzis - Could be out at night.  His CR means he's not afraid of much and his dragon blood may be mischievous
	- Yingman - Home safe
	- Stefania - Only because she's responsible for security.
	- Glour - Night watchman.  The lead in this scene warning to players to beware and may refer players to other NPCs/locations when relevant. 
	- Obur - Also on-call for security
	- Kaelen - A possible encounter after hours and mediated by Glour
