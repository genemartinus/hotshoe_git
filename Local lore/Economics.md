Frontier towns are all driven by sustenance and material goods as they struggle to establish order.  Hotshoe's economy barely exists and that is what makes it so compelling.  Settlers with aspiration to secure their future comfort are enthusiastic, ambitious, competitive, secretive, conspirators, suspicious and form fragile partnerships.

Each of these items, ambitions and processes illustrate the relationships with water, ash, craftmanship, danger, life forms and sincere self centered interest to make changes for the better while developing a deeper understanding of what the hell is going on.
