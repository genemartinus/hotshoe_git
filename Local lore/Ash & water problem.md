The problem of the heat/mineral based local ecology and human needs for carbon based resources that depend on water.  Ash and water affects almost everything.  See [[Thermal based life forms]]

[[Millicent]] and [[Dimble]] and [[Milton]] and [[Phanzis]] all have their interests and needs for clean water  parallel with the reality of life in the new carbon/thermal ecosystem of Hotshoe.

[[Jube]] and [[Kaelen]] have opposing interpretations of the implications of how the landscape has been altered in unexpected ways from the eruption and persistent ash falls.  The crux of their dispute is at the cemeteries ([[Hotshoe cemetery]] & [[Dwarven cemetery]]) where appearance of the ash fungus seems to portend something much more serious; as if all the other new plants and animal/monsters weren't bad enough.  Bringing in the dead always ups the ante and gives nightmares.  

These stand offs divide and unite the residents of Hotshoe and give the players reasons to engage with the hamlet, residents and challenges while they try to figure out which of the new disturbances and occurrences might possibly be worthy of a reward for new chemical ingredients to improve magical conducive ink.
