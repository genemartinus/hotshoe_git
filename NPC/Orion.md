# **Orion. Obsidian scavenger and merchant

#### **Statistics**

**Medium Human, Chaotic Neutral**
    
- **Armor Class:** 14 (Patchwork Leather)
- **Hit Points:** 13 ($3d8$)
- **Speed:** 30 ft.

| **STR** | **DEX** | **CON** | **INT** | **WIS** | **CHA** |
| ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- |
| 10 (+0) | 16 (+3) | 12 (+1) | 11 (+0) | 14 (+2) | 10 (+0) |

- **Skills:** Athletics +2, Perception +4, Sleight of Hand +5, Stealth +5, Survival +4
- **Senses:** Passive Perception 14
- **Languages:** Common, Thieves' Cant
- **Challenge:** 1/2 (100 XP) | **Proficiency Bonus:** +2

#### **Special Traits**
- **Cunning Action:** On each of his turns, Orion can use a bonus action to take the Dash, Disengage, or Hide action.
- **Sneak Attack (1/Turn):** Orion deals an extra `dice: 1d` damage when he hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally that isn't incapacitated.
- **Sure-Footed Scavenger:** Orion has **Advantage** on Dexterity (Acrobatics) checks made to navigate difficult terrain consisting of jagged rocks, glass, or unstable ash slopes.
- **Glass-Scarred Hands:** Orion's hands are a map of calluses and old cuts. He is immune to damage from handling non-magical obsidian or glass shards.

#### **Combat & Actions**

- **Obsidian Jagger (Dagger):** _Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack:_ +5 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. _Hit:_  `dice: 1d4+3`piercing damage.
- **Bag of Shards:** _Ranged Weapon Attack:_ +5 to hit, range 15/30 ft., one target. _Hit:_ `dice: 1d4+3` slashing damage. Orion reaches into his collection bag and hurls a handful of raw, razor-sharp obsidian.
- **Dust Kick:** _Melee Manual Attack:_ +5 to hit. Orion kicks a cloud of hot ash at a creature within 5 feet. The target must succeed on a DC 11 Constitution saving throw or have **Disadvantage** on its next attack roll before the end of its next turn.

#### **Roleplaying Notes**
- **Personality:** Fidgety and alert. He talks quickly, constantly glancing at the volcano's peak as if expecting it to throw more "merchandise" at him. He values "the find" above all else.
- **Visuals:** His clothes are singed at the hems and reinforced with thick leather patches at the knees and elbows. He wears heavy goggles pushed up onto a soot-stained forehead.
- **Voice:** A raspy, breathless whisper. He sounds like he’s always trying to keep a secret or hide from a predator.

#### **Specific storyline**
Orion is an obsidian scavenger.  The vacuum that emerged in in the obsidian trade when the dwarven encampment and mines were buried opened opportunities for hum and his crew, [[Durn]] and [[Kip]].  They operate at the edge of Hotshoe's social interactions because they don't want anything to disturb their corner on the market.  The locals mostly ignore them because they aren't causing trouble and sometimes have money.  They also aren't full time residents because it is a long ways to carry their product to market in one of the larger settlements. 
[[Stefania]] actually welcomes their presence because they are extra eyes and ears on what's happening in the Hotshoe valley.

#### **Logical and moral implications**
Orion has little interest in the rules of law or civil society except he knows upsetting the balance of order is not favorable for business.  In this way, Orion might be persuaded to do things that run counter to the norms if there's little chance of being caught and has reward for the effort.  Translation: physical work, hardship and information are what he is willing to barter.  The players might try to make friends and that is an uphill battle.

#### **Reward/treasure/favor**

- **The Favor:** If the party rescues Orion from a crisis in town or out in the volcanic wasteland, he will show them the secret of his "Glass Paths" that will reduce their movement in the volcanic wasteland by half.
- **The Loot:** A heavy leather pouch containing 22 gp worth of raw obsidian shards, a small jar of "Ash-Balm" (soothes burns), two bottles of [[Hot yam fireproof boot polish]] and a single perfectly clear obsidian flake that could be used as a magnifying glass.


Orion is a "Glass Cannon" in the most literal sense. With only 13 HP, he is incredibly fragile, but that Sneak Attack (95 damage) is an absolute nuclear option. If he hits, he likely kills anything short of a high-level boss instantly.
Because of this, Orion should play like a paranoid ghost: never seen, never touched, but utterly lethal if ignored.
Combat Strategy: The Obsidian Ghost
1. The "One-Shot" Condition
Orion's entire existence revolves around triggering that 95 damage. To do this, he needs Advantage or an Ally within 5 feet of his target.
 * The Meat Shields: Orion should never initiate combat alone. He needs Durn, Kip, or an environment (like jagged rocks) to distract the party.
 * Cunning Hide: On every single turn, Orion should use his Bonus Action to Hide. He strikes from the shadows, then vanishes back into the ash and obsidian.
2. Terrain Mastery: The "Obsidian Floor"
Orion’s Sure-Footed Scavenger trait is his greatest defensive tool.
 * Lure into Hazard: He should fight in areas filled with jagged glass or unstable slopes (Difficult Terrain). While the players struggle and potentially take damage, Orion moves at full speed and with Advantage to stay upright.
 * Glass-Scarred Hands: He can literally climb or hide inside piles of razor-sharp obsidian that would shred anyone else. Use this to create "safe zones" where melee players cannot follow him without taking damage.
3. Tactical Rotation: "Dust and Dagger"
If Orion is cornered or forced into a confrontation:
 * The Setup (Dust Kick): If an enemy gets in his face, he uses Dust Kick. This imposes Disadvantage on the enemy, giving Orion a better chance to survive the next swing.
 * The Escape (Cunning Disengage): After kicking dust, he uses his Bonus Action to Disengage and runs 30 ft away.
 * The Kill (Obsidian Jagger): From a distance, he throws his dagger at a target currently engaged with one of his allies to trigger the massive Sneak Attack damage.

Important Mechanical Note: The "95"
> Heads up for the DM: That 95 Sneak Attack damage is astronomically high for a CR 1/2 creature. A single hit will "Power Word Kill" almost any player character under Level 10.
>  * If this is a typo: You might want to adjust it to 3d6 (approx. 10 damage).
>  * If this is intentional: Treat Orion as a Legendary Assassin. He is a high-priority target that the players must find and neutralize before he gets a single clear shot.
> 
Roleplay Flavor: The Scavenger's Grudge
 * Desperate Survival: Orion doesn't fight for glory; he fights for "Black Gold" (obsidian). If he sees he can't win, he will use Cunning Action: Dash to disappear into the ash wastes.
 * Environmental Interaction: He might intentionally collapse a shelf of glass or a pile of obsidian shards to create a barrier between himself and the party.
