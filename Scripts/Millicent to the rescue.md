**Scene**: A tense, muddy intersection on the outskirts of Hotshoe. The party is pressed against a broken cart, surrounded by two persistent, shuffling zombies. One adventurer (Player A) is trying to reload a crossbow with shaking hands, while another (Player B) is attempting to hold a shield against a clumsy, rotting strike. The air is heavy with the smell of wet earth and decay.

**Narrative**: The shadows of the encroaching woods seem to conspire against you. The zombies, though slow, are relentless in their hunger. Just as it seems Player B's shield arm will give way under the weight of a powerful, clumsy swing from the first zombie, a high, commanding voice cuts through the groans of the undead.

**Millicent:**  (Shouting with a fierce, abrasive edge) "Hold! Hold your damn ground, you blundering fools!"

**Player B** (Startled, momentarily looking away from the zombie) "Who—?"

**Narrative** Millicent bursts from a nearby copse of trees, emerging with an explosion of aggressive energy. She is a towering, gaunt figure in dark, embroidered leathers. A magnificent, feathered tan hat is pulled low over her intense hazel eyes, which are burning with focused rage. She doesn’t slow down; she charges.

**Narrative**  With a sharp, fluid motion, Millicent throws her entire weight forward, shoulder-checking the first zombie (the one threatening User B) with raw force. It stumbles backward into the mud with a wet slap. The second zombie shifts its empty gaze toward her, groaning.

**Player A** (Finally cocking the crossbow) "Look out! The other one!"

**Millicent** (A commanding Scottish snap, not even breaking stride as she moves past User B) "I _can_ count, you prattling amateur! Get that ridiculous weapon cocked or throw it at them! Move!"

**Narrative**  Millicent closes the distance to the second zombie. As it clumsily raises its arms to grab her, she makes her choice. A heavy, ceramic vial, prepped and ready in her gloved hand, is thrown.

**Narrative**  Shatter!_ The **Alchemical Flask** (Acid) explodes directly against the zombie's chest. Thick, acrid smoke immediate erupts, sizzling against the dead flesh. The zombie reels, momentarily blind and distracted by the sudden, burning pain.

**Millicent**(Stopping a few feet away, glaring at the party while aggressively scrubbing a sticky residue off her mortar and pestle) "And _that_, you utterly helpless lot, is how you deal with trash that can't feel pain. It _burns_ them. Idiots. _Abrasive Shield_ triggers; she glares with such condescension that the party feels smaller than the zombies."

**Millicent**  (Turning her commanding glare to User A) "And _you_! Why is your powder still dry? My guide will tell you that _spare the dying_ is for people who use their actual magic, not for people who can't even count to _one shot_ before panicking. _Experimental Fortitude (Second Wind)_ triggers; she takes a deep, grounding breath, resetting her intense focus."

**Narrative** The first zombie begins to stir in the mud. The second one, its face hideously scarred by the acid, is roaring silently.

**Millicent** (Her voice dropping, the command absolute) "Right. You lot. Back against the cart. Now. I’ve done the clever bit. _You_ finish them, unless you want your next healing potion to taste like goblin sweat. I have a shop to open and zero patience for incompetence. Move! That's an order!"
