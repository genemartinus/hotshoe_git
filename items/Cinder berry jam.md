A delicacy [[Kaelen]] makes with cinder berries.  He's particularly keen because he feels this fulfills part of his interpretation of Nerull's final solution where the living are being fed by the fruits of the mechanisms that are products and evidence (for Kaelen) of the inevitable.  The jam is a real success given that [[Kaelen]] has learned how to render the jam edible by mixing it with needle grass, letting it grow and then straining out.  Curious that other citizens like [[Dimble]] are working to achieve the same success with their efforts to coexist with the environment today.
