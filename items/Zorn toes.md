Your intuition for "Second Level Loot" is spot on. At this stage, items move from being simple consumables to Utility Tools that fundamentally change how the players interact with your world.
A Xorn’s Toe (properly curated) is a fantastic specialized dowsing rod. It doesn't break the game by giving them infinite gold, but it creates a "mini-game" of exploration that fits perfectly with the Hotshoe scavenger vibe.
💎 The Xorn’s "Mineral Twitch" (Curated Toe)
Wondrous Item, Uncommon
This is a calcified, three-taloned digit from a Xorn Youngling, cured in Ash Yam Sap and polished with Needle Grass to preserve its innate connection to the earth.
1. Passive Characteristics
 * The Twitch: While the toe is worn on a belt or held in a hand, the user gains a +2 bonus to Passive Perception specifically for detecting the presence of precious metals and gemstones.
 * The "Tell": When a gemstone (worth at least 10gp) is within 30 feet, the toe begins to twitch or vibrate rhythmically. It does not point; it simply pulses faster the closer you get, like a Geiger counter for wealth.
2. Active Search Mechanics
To actually find the prize once the "Twitch" starts, the player must engage with the environment:
 * The Wisdom (Survival/Perception) Check: Used to "read" the pulse. A successful check tells the player if the gem is above ground, buried in ash, or embedded in a rock vein.
 * The Intelligence (Investigation/Nature) Check: Used to deduce where a gem would naturally (or unnaturally) be.
   * Example: "There is a cluster of Needle Grass here; the iron in the soil likely hid a Garnet deposit."
 * The Xorn’s Curse: If the player is also carrying Orion’s Magnetite Polish, the toe becomes "confused." The magnetic pull of the boots interferes with the mineral scent, giving Disadvantage on the search checks.
🛠️ The Curation Process (Why it's "Level 2")
A raw Xorn toe is just a stinking piece of elemental rock. To make it "Properly Curated," the players need to utilize the town's experts:
 * Harvesting: Requires a DC 15 Dexterity (Sleight of Hand) check to remove the toe without shattering the delicate internal "scent-filaments."
 * The "Cold-Soak": The toe must be soaked in Millicent’s specialized "Brine-Neutralizer" for 24 hours to stop it from trying to "glide" back into the earth.
 * The Tuning: Durn must use his Mason's Tools to etch specific geomantic runes into the talon so it "speaks" to the user's hand.
🔍 Higher-Level Scaling (The "Aged" Toe)
As the players reach Level 3 or 4, they might find ways to upgrade the loot:
 * Resonant Ink: If Phanzis applies his Conducive Ink to the runes, the toe’s range increases to 60 feet.
 * The "Hungry" Toe: If the player "feeds" the toe a small diamond (worth 50gp), the toe becomes hyper-attuned to that specific mineral for 24 hours, allowing them to ignore common quartz and find only high-value gems.
📜 DM Plot Hook: "The False Positive"
Since the toe senses minerals, it might start twitching uncontrollably near a Scald-Scale Serpent (due to the Salt-Gems in its throat) or a Fire Snake (due to the obsidian shards in its blood). This forces the players to decide: Is the twitching a treasure, or is it a predator?
