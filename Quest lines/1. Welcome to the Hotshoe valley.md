## Local NPCs meet the players

- **Introduction to Hotshoe**
	- The characters arrive at the crest of the valley/watershed and get their first look at the valley. Several things are visible (depending on the time of day):  Mt. Conniption volcano to the north behind the glacier and lahar path from the last eruption, to the south is a dwarven cemetery, further down the valley is the salt lake and in the distance is a small town, river and bridge.
	- A group of villagers are approaching the road from the direction of the Dwarven cemetery.  They have shovels and buckets with shoulder-yokes.
	- **Encounters**
		- **[[Stefania]]** greets the players.  She's the one who minds Hotshoe's perimeter and oversees the day/night guard shifts by Obur and Glour.  In this role she is more familiar with the landscape and the deadly foes. ![[Stefania.png|283]]
		- **Conversation starters** 
			- STARTS OUT SUSPICIOUS WITH THE STANDARD 'FRIEND OR FOE', BRANDISHING WEAPONS, 'WHAT IS YOUR BUSINESS HERE'. STAND DOWN....
				- The content or reason for the guarded welcome serves to alert the players about some of the challenges Hotshoe faces.
			- There are few travelers on this path, most keep to the road to the capital {name} to the east.
			- Hotshoe is that way, description of the town and characteristics.
			- The details and history surrounding the dwarven cemetery and how the landscape/people have changed.  Refers to [[Yingman]] to find out more.
		- **[[Obur]]** chimes in that he knows the history of the dwarves and will entertain the conversation at the [[Thirsty Pebble]].  He wants to get back to town and his job as watchman and pass the torch (literally) to [[Glour]] who takes care of things at night.
		- **[[Durn]]** is worried about the safety of the group and advises caution along the way to town or wherever the players are going.  He might hint there are many creatures to beware of.
		- **[[Jube]]** can describe the condition of the dwarf cemetery and this hooks into the problems the town faces given the ash fall and how the town is coping.  She observes concern over changes in the cemetery that foreshadow the relationship of [[Kaelen]] and his harvest.  Comments that he may have to come up here to see it for himself.
- **Return to the cemetery**
	- Returning to the cemetery will be to gather evidence of the [[Kaelen]]/ash phenomenon and contribute to the players' understandings of what Hotshoe is facing.
- **Debacle at the cemetery**
	- There is an [[Ash spore husk]] eruption that takes place here after the conflict at the Hotshoe cemetery is resolved.  The residents get their pitchforks and rush up the trail.
