## Hooks

There isn't any lodging in Hotshoe.  The Players are on their own to ingratiate themselves with the residents in hopes they might have a place to rest.  This fact is obvious to everyone in a sort of 
		- They have their reasons to be here and are not going away for at least awhile.
		- What are we going to do with them? 
		- They might be useful.  
		- They might get into trouble with the local flora and fauna until they know what they're up against.

A volunteer might offer to put them up for a night hoping they can get a favor in return.  This is a chance to force the players into a quest or at least make a choice of what happens next and might be couched as an opportunity to advance their main reasons for being in Hotshoe.

An alternative is the players are offered use of an abandoned yet serviceable house in hopes they might like to purchase it an stay awhile if they prove useful.
