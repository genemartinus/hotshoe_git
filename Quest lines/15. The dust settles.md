### After aftermath
Resolution of the fungus-spore-cemetery conflict weaves together with original quests to get new ingredients from the Hotshoe area for making magical ink is still on-going; the ash-fungus-spore is available for the players to investigate.  

The players develop or are given the idea to investigate the prospects of the ash fungus that Kaelen has been collecting.  They recover a stash or Kaelen shows then how to harvest it themselves during an initial encounter where he asks for their help.  Millicent is the obvious ally here because of her craft.  Her role would be to render the spores stable to preserve their qualities so they might be applied in the ink craft experiments by the ink experts.  The refined ash-fungus-spore dust may have some qualities making is valuable in itself - glow in the dark liquid light (think frodo's vial of light) and the prospect of sloshing glowing liquid around in a dark place is an amusing idea.  Properties of ash-spore dust - mild glow in the dark as a solid or as powder packed tightly to reduce reaction with the air, sprinkled to mix with air produces a luminescent cloud that can approximate the light from a candle or torch depending on quantity, mixed with water produces a brighter and shorter effect with other possibilities as a Molotov cocktail of illumination (requires prep materials and action time).  Players are then motivated to find out who is giving the reward for new chemically active magic ink ingredients.
