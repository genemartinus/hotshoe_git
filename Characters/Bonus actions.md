## **Universal "Two-Weapon" Rule**

If any character (like **Harsent** or **Brevil**) is holding two light melee weapons (like two daggers or a scimitar/shortsword combo):

- **Trigger:** You take the **Attack Action** with your main-hand weapon.
    
- **Bonus Action:** You can make **one melee attack** with the off-hand weapon.
    

> **Note for the DM:** Remember that most characters do _not_ add their ability modifier (like +3) to the **damage** of this second attack unless they have the "Two-Weapon Fighting" style (which **Harsent** does!).
