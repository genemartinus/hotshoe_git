## **Harsent (Level 1 Fighter)**

_To scale Harsent, we reduce his massive +3 Constitution and slightly lower his Dexterity to fit a standard "starting array" (Point Buy/Standard Array)._

- **AC:** 14 (Padded) | **HP:** 12 ($1d10 + 2$) | **Speed:** 30 ft.
    
- **STR:** 10 (+0), **DEX:** 16 (+3), **CON:** 14 (+2), **INT:** 9 (-1), **WIS:** 9 (-1), **CHA:** 9 (-1)
    
- **Skills:** Acrobatics +5, Intimidation +1.
    
- **Saving Throws:** STR +2, CON +4.
    
- **Abilities:**
    
    - **Second Wind:** (1/Short Rest) Regain $1d10 + 1$ HP.
        
    - **Fighting Style (Two-Weapon Fighting):** When you engage in two-weapon fighting, you can add your ability modifier to the damage of the second attack.
        
- **Actions:**
    
    - **Scimitar:** +5 to hit, reach 5 ft. **Hit:** 6 ($1d6 + 3$) slashing.
        
    - **Shortsword (Off-hand):** +5 to hit, reach 5 ft. **Hit:** 6 ($1d6 + 3$) piercing.
        
- **Scaled Down:** We removed **Action Surge** and **Backflip** (save those for Level 2 and 3 archetype choices).

## **Thomasine (Level 1 Ranger)**

_Thomasine’s +7 damage was a "boss-level" stat. We’ve brought her back to the standard +3 DEX modifier and shifted her detection to the Level 1 Ranger "Favored Enemy" feature._

- **AC:** 14 (Leather) | **HP:** 11 ($1d10 + 1$) | **Speed:** 30 ft.
    
- **STR:** 10 (+0), **DEX:** 16 (+3), **CON:** 12 (+1), **INT:** 11 (+0), **WIS:** 14 (+2), **CHA:** 8 (-1)
    
- **Skills:** Perception +4, Survival +4, Insight +4.
    
- **Saving Throws:** STR +2, DEX +5.
    
- **Abilities:**
    
    - **Favored Enemy (Extra-Planar):** Advantage on Survival checks to track Elementals/Aberrations and Intelligence checks to recall info about them.
        
    - **Natural Explorer (Volcanic/Mountain):** Doubled proficiency on Int/Wis checks related to your home terrain.
        
- **Actions:**
    
    - **Rapier:** +5 to hit, reach 5 ft. **Hit:** 7 ($1d8 + 3$) piercing.
        
    - **Blowgun:** +5 to hit, range 25/100. **Hit:** 4 piercing (1 fixed + 3 DEX).
        
- **Scaled Down:** Removed **Horde Breaker** and spellcasting. She is now a dedicated scout waiting for her magic to manifest.

## **Assunta (Level 1 Rogue)**

_Assunta’s "Backflip" and low health make her perfect as a Level 1 Rogue, using Cunning Action and Sneak Attack to stay relevant without needing a high AC._

- **AC:** 12 (Leather) | **HP:** 8 ($1d8 + 0$) | **Speed:** 35 ft.
    
- **STR:** 8 (-1), **DEX:** 14 (+2), **CON:** 10 (+0), **INT:** 12 (+1), **WIS:** 14 (+2), **CHA:** 12 (+1)
    
- **Skills:** Acrobatics +4, Investigation +3, Insight +4, Stealth +4.
    
- **Saving Throws:** DEX +4, INT +3.
    
- **Abilities:**
    
    - **Sneak Attack:** Once per turn, deal an extra $1d6$ damage to a creature you hit with an advantage or if an ally is within 5 ft of it.
        
    - **Expertise (Acrobatics/Insight):** Doubled proficiency in these skills.
        
- **Actions:**
    
    - **Whip:** +4 to hit, reach 10 ft. **Hit:** 4 ($1d4 + 2$) slashing.
        
    - **Sling:** +4 to hit, range 30/120. **Hit:** 4 ($1d4 + 2$) bludgeoning.
        
- **Scaled Down:** Removed **Action Surge**. Her "Backflip" is now represented by high **Acrobatics Expertise**, allowing her to move through combat effectively.
