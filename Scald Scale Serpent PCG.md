- **Action Economy**: Area-of-Effect (AoE) focused. **Salt Spray** provides a high-impact "Control" channel, while **Boiling Bite** handles single-target termination.
    
- **Correction Noted**: Attack modifier standardized to **+4** (Dex 14 + PB 2). **Salt Spray** damage and **Boiling Bite** damage dice adjusted for CR 1/2 balance.

## Section I: Say hello to the Brine Serpent

The Brine Serpent is a **Hydro-Thermal Apex Trapper**. Your "Signal" is one of **Corrosive Agitation**—you operate as a **Brute** that thrives in high-viscosity environments. Unlike the previous squad members, you are an elemental force of nature; you don't negotiate, you dissolve. You are designed to drag targets into the "Void" of deep saltwater, using your **Blindsight** to navigate while your enemies are blinded by your boiling excretions.

---

## Section II: Tactical Assessment: The Turn Domain

- **👣 Footprint**: 55ft Engagement Diameter (40ft Swim + 15ft Salt Spray cone).
    
- **🌌 Center of Gravity**: **Fluid (Ambush)**. You function best when submerged. Your **Saltwater Camouflage** allows you to "Ghost" into the environment, making you a hidden hazard until you strike.
    
- **⚖️ Leverage**: **Condition Locking**. By applying **Blinded** via Salt Spray, you generate Advantage for your **Boiling Bite** and force Disadvantage on the enemy's retaliatory strikes.
    
- **⚠️ Weakness**: **Thermal Dehydration**. Your **Water-Dependent** trait makes you a liability in dry land-based "Arid" theaters. If your AC drops to 11, your defensive envelope becomes porous.
    

---

## Section III: Navigational Narrative

- ✊ **Melee (5ft): The Death Zone**. This is where **Brine Skin** shines. Every time a target hits you, they risk 2 (1d4) acid damage. You win "Grind" fights by simply existing.
    
- 🔨 **The Gap (10–15ft): The Scald Zone**. Your primary "Impact" theater. Position yourself to catch multiple targets in your **Salt Spray** cone.
    
- 🏹 **Ranged (15ft+): The Home Zone**. Non-functional. You have no ranged projectiles. You must use your 40ft swim speed to bridge this gap instantly.
    
- 🌪️ **Survival (The Void): The Reset**. If Salt Spray fails to recharge, submerge. Use the water's depth to break line-of-sight and utilize your 30ft **Blindsight** to track the enemy's vibrations while you remain hidden.

## Section V: Do this to get the job done

### ☀️ ROBUST (Full Readiness)

**I. 🔵 SETUP: Brackish Ambush**

- **Best Use**: Gaining the initiative before the enemy can deploy shields.
    
- Submerge in saltwater. Use **Saltwater Camouflage** (+4 Stealth with Advantage) to approach within 15ft of the party's backline.
    

**II. 🔴 IMPACT: Scalding Eruption**

- **Best Use**: Blinding the "Cannons" (Thomasine/Emerick) to disrupt their accuracy.
    
- Deploy **Salt Spray** (Action). Target at least two enemies. If they fail the DC 11 Dex save, they are Blinded and taking fire damage.
    

**III. 🟡 RECOVERY: Deep-Water Drag**

- **Best Use**: Isolating a target for termination.
    
- Use your 40ft Swim speed to move adjacent to a Blinded target. Force them to deal with your **Brine Skin** on their turn.
    

### ⛈️ STORMY (Resource Strain)

**IV. 🔵 SETUP: Serpent Coil**

- **Best Use**: Maximizing survival during a recharge delay.
    
- Move to the flank of the combat. Take the **Hide** action under the surface. Use your **Blindsight** to keep eyes on the "Signal."
    

**V. 🔴 IMPACT: Boiling Incision**

- **Best Use**: Focusing damage on a single "Sink" (Obur/Hara).
    
- Use **Boiling Bite** (+4). The combination of Piercing and Fire damage bypasses basic resistances like the Dwarf’s poison resistance.
    

**VI. 🟡 RECOVERY: Caustic Feedback**

- **Best Use**: Turning the enemy's aggression into their own downfall.
    
- Stay within 5ft of attackers. Every melee hit you take forces a DC 11 Con save or **2 acid damage**.
    

### 🌑 DEPLETED (Low Resources)

**VII. 🔵 SETUP: Tidal Breach**

- **Best Use**: Closing the gap when speed is reduced.
    
- Use your 20ft Land speed to reach the nearest body of water. Without hydration, your AC will soon drop to 11.
    

**VIII. 🔴 IMPACT: Desperate Snap**

- **Best Use**: A final strike before retreating.
    
- Standard **Boiling Bite**. If it hits, the target takes both physical and thermal damage.
    

**IX. 🟡 RECOVERY: Silt Retreat**

- **Best Use**: Total system extraction.
    
- Use the **Dash** action (80ft total swim) to move into the dark depths where you can hide and rehydrate.

The Brine Serpent's strategy is a byproduct of **Atmospheric Pressure**. Its **Blindsight** and **Stealth Advantage** make it the master of the "Low-Information" environment. Its taxonomy as a **Brute** is earned through **Brine Skin**—a feature that ensures the Serpent deals damage even when it isn't taking its turn. It is the perfect counter to a melee-heavy squad.
